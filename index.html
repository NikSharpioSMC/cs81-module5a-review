Hobby Tracker

This is a simple hobby tracker application that allows you to log your hobbies, the time spent on them, and your mood during the sessions.

Features:

  • Log hobbies with time and mood
  • Calculate total time spent on hobbies
  • Count unique hobbies
  • Identify long sessions based on time threshold
  • Count sessions based on mood
